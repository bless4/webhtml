Services


Our team’s motto is “We challenge status quo to make life less complicated for people” as we like to drive change and take the lead in making daily life less complex and more fun at work. By doing so we want to enable our people and teams to become as good as possible and thereby enable improved customer experience every day – both internally and externally! Our team has numerous exciting challenges ahead related to the Digitalisation of Cars in general and in the areas of Autonomous Drive, Mobility, Infotainment & Connectivity and Electrification in specific. If you are the right person for this job, you will realise the opportunity to play a key role for the company’s performance in this area. You’ll see how using how using the right tools, methods, process and your support to the management team will drive positive change in all part of the company. Is this something that sounds exciting? Then you will thrive and excel as our Business Administrative Assistant within SW & Electronics Procurement.
